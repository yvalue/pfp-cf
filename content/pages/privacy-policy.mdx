## Introduction

Welcome to **AI-PFP**. We value your privacy and are committed to explaining clearly and transparently how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your information. This Privacy Policy applies to our data handling practices when you visit the AI-PFP website, create an account, upload photos, generate avatars, or otherwise use our related services.

By using AI-PFP, you acknowledge that you have read and understood the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please stop using our services.

## Information We Collect

To provide, maintain, and improve AI-PFP, we may collect the following categories of information:

### 1. Account and Identity Information

When you register, sign in, or contact us, we may collect:

- Your name, nickname, or public display name
- Your email address
- Authentication-related information, including information provided by third-party sign-in services
- Any other information you choose to submit to us

### 2. Uploaded Content and Generated Results

When you use AI-PFP to generate avatars, headshots, or other image-based content, we may process:

- Photos, profile images, or reference images that you upload
- Prompts, style preferences, and generation parameters you submit
- Images, thumbnails, or other output generated by the system for you

This information is necessary for AI-PFP to provide its core functionality. Without it, we generally cannot complete the image generation service.

### 3. Payment and Order Information

If you purchase paid features, credits, or subscriptions, we may collect transaction-related information such as:

- Order numbers and transaction status
- Product or plan details
- Billing-related information
- Necessary records returned by payment channels or processors

We generally do not directly store complete bank card numbers or similar sensitive payment details. Such information is typically processed by third-party payment providers under their own policies.

### 4. Usage Data, Device Information, and Logs

When you access or use AI-PFP, we may automatically collect:

- IP address
- Browser type, device type, operating system, and language settings
- Pages visited, time spent, click behavior, and referral information
- Login times, operation logs, error logs, and security event records

This information helps us maintain service stability, detect abuse, and improve user experience.

### 5. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We may use cookies, local storage, and similar technologies to:

- Maintain login sessions and security
- Remember your preferences
- Measure traffic and analyze product usage
- Improve functionality, content, and marketing effectiveness

You can usually manage or disable cookies through your browser settings, but doing so may affect the availability or functionality of certain parts of the service.

### 6. Communications Information

When you email us, submit support requests, start a customer service conversation, or send feedback, we may keep:

- Your contact details
- The content of your communications and attachments
- Records of how the issue was handled

## How We Use Information

We may use the information we collect for the following purposes:

- To provide AI-PFP account, generation, payment, order, and customer support features
- To process your uploaded content and deliver generated image results
- To authenticate users and protect account and transaction security
- To manage subscriptions, payments, refunds, promotions, or credit grants
- To respond to your questions, requests, and complaints
- To monitor service performance, troubleshoot issues, and prevent fraud, abuse, and other unlawful activity
- To analyze product usage and improve features, model workflows, and the overall experience
- To retain and use relevant information when required for legal compliance, dispute handling, or protection of rights
- To send important service-related notices, such as policy updates, order status, or security alerts

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

To operate AI-PFP, we may share necessary information with third-party vendors that help us provide the service, such as:

These third parties are permitted to process relevant information only to the extent necessary to provide services for us.

### 2. For Legal Compliance or Protection of Legitimate Interests

Where reasonably necessary, we may disclose relevant information to:

- Comply with applicable laws, regulations, court orders, or government requests

If AI-PFP is involved in a reorganization, financing, merger, acquisition, asset sale, or similar transaction, relevant information may be disclosed or transferred as part of that transaction to the extent permitted and necessary under applicable law.

We retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy and, where required, for additional periods under applicable law. For example:
